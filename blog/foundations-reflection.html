Foundations Reflections

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

  1. Learning about growth mindset and neuroplasticity was really interesting to me as this helped me understand that I could achieve my goals and learn new skills. The imposter syndrome feeling is still there but it is not as loud with the knowledge that I can rewire my brain.
  2. I have had many ups and downs during foundations as I got covid and I still have some lingering symptoms which impact on my learning. I have found that spending time each morning to set intentions and plan out my day with time and goals has been useful.
  3. I have learnt not to compare myself to others as much as I did when I began. Understanding that everyone comes from a different background and experience.

What are the roles of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values - understanding where you are and where you are going and what for.

Empathy - being able to see things from another person's point of view.

Self-awareness - understanding your own strengths and weaknesses.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

I was surprised by my learning of emotional intelligence, not because I didn't know what it was but because I didn't really understand it and how important it is. I enjoyed learning about it and how I can increase my EQ through mindfulness and repetitiveness.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Emotional Intelligence, I feel this is something that I can really work on. While learning something new it is easy to fall into the thoughts of “I can’t do this” but this is where I want to work on shifting and challenging these thoughts.
